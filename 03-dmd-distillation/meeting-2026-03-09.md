### VRAM Constraint (Important Finding)

- Methods with **discriminator** (DMD2, f-distill, LADD) cannot run on 4x RTX 5090 32GB
- Methods **without discriminator** (ECT, CD) fit well at ~25-30 GB/GPU
- This is a known limitation for Wan2.1-1.3B with standard video resolution (832x480, 81 frames)
